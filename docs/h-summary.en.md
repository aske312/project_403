# Project_403

Project_403 is one communication space for people and teams. It combines the speed of a personal messenger with the power of work platforms: direct chats, groups, channels, calls and conferences will be available in one app.

## Who It Is For

### Personal Communication

- direct conversations between users;
- quick search by name or login;
- groups for friends, family, study and communities;
- channels for posts, news and announcements.

### Corporate Communication

- team and department chats;
- group discussions for projects;
- company, team and product channels;
- conferences, audio calls and video calls without switching to another service.

## What Project_403 Gives You

### Communication In One Place

- direct chats like Telegram;
- workspaces and team discussions like Slack;
- voice rooms, group calls and video conferences like Discord;
- one user profile for both personal and work communication.

### Easy Start

- simple registration and login;
- email confirmation to protect the account;
- long user session so people do not have to sign in every day;
- clear hints when creating an account and choosing a password.

## Roadmap

### Upcoming Features

- user list and quick start for direct chats;
- direct and group chats;
- channels for broad announcements;
- message history and delivery states.

### Next Level

- audio and video calls;
- group calls;
- video conferences;
- tools for corporate spaces, roles and access rights.
